About K-Pasa

K-pasa Curaçao is a weekly cultural-event-guide and has been published on Curaçao since 1997. Every week since, it has shown a weekly overview of things to do on the Island. We never missed a week! K-pasa is a handy overview of dining, entertainment, parties, happy hours and the newest movies of the week. The cultural agenda is popular with tourists and locals.
